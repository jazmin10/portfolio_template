About Me

Hi, welcome to my site! Yes, that's me. I am a web developer with a simple mind creating full-stack websites. I am currently paving my way into the web development world. I decided to learn how to code because unlike other fields, when you are done coding you can display your results. You can interact with your application. Others can interact with your application! There is a joy when arriving to a coherent solution!

My past experience includes serving as a Site Manager for Foundation Communities, an organization that provides its community with programs for affordable housing, financial stability, health-care services, and on-site education programs. I also worked as a Business Analyst for UT Austin's ID Center and a Membership Coordinator for Workers Defense Project.

In my off-time, I am a fan of movie nights with popcorn, as well as soccer watch parties.

I graduated with a Bachelor's in Math from the University of Texas at Austin and soon I will be completing a full-stack web development certification from UT Austin Coding Bootcamp.
